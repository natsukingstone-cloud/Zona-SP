About us

Tucked away in the heart of Asakusa, Zo-na is a cozy haven serving sweets crafted from strawberries grown in the warm onsen soil of Zao. Each bite captures nature’s gentle sweetness and the care of the people who nurture it. Relax, smile, and enjoy a little moment of happiness with us.

浅草のまんなかで、蔵王のいちごを使ったスイーツをお届けしています。いちご一粒一粒にこめられたやさしさとぬくもりを、そのまま感じていただけるように。笑顔になれる、ほんのひとときをどうぞ。
